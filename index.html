Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
Getting started is easy! Simply browse our vocabulary lessons, word lists, and practice exercises. You can begin at the beginner level or choose a topic that suits your current skills. Each lesson includes examples, quizzes, and pronunciation support.
2. Is this website free to use?
Some features, like daily word tracking and progress reports, may require a free account. However, you can still access many lessons and word lists without signing up.
3. Is this website suitable for beginners?
Yes! Our lessons are designed for learners of all levels. If you’re a beginner, you’ll find simple word lists, easy examples, and step-by-step practice to build your vocabulary.
4. How can I improve my vocabulary faster?
The best way is to practice regularly. Use our daily word challenge, take quizzes, and try to make sentences with new words you learn. Repetition and practice are the keys to improvement.
5. Can I practice pronunciation here?
Yes! Many of our lessons include audio examples so you can hear how each word is pronounced and practice along.
6. Is the website free to use?
Most of our content is free. Some advanced features, like personalized learning paths or premium word packs, may require an upgrade, but basic vocabulary lessons are always free.
7. Can I use this website on my phone?
Absolutely! Our website is mobile-friendly, so you can learn new words anytime, anywhere on your smartphone or tablet.
